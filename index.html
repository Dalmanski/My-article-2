MY GACHA GAMES I'VE PLAYED
Wuthering Waves
Wuthering Waves is a free-to-play open-world action role-playing game developed by Kuro Games. Players explore a vast, futuristic world, battling enemies with various weapons and abilities. The game features a gacha system for obtaining characters and weapons, and offers a variety of quests, challenges, and exploration opportunities.
Honkai: Star Rail
Honkai: Star Rail is a turn-based role-playing game developed by HoYoverse. Players embark on a cosmic journey aboard the Astral Express, exploring diverse planets and battling enemies using unique character abilities. The game features a gacha system for collecting characters and weapons, and offers a rich storyline, engaging combat, and stunning visuals.
Reverse: 1999
Reverse: 1999 is a turn-based tactical RPG set in a time-looping world. Players assume the role of a Timekeeper, traveling through different eras to uncover the mystery behind the "Storm" that has reversed time. The game features strategic combat, character customization, and a unique time-bending mechanic.
Zenless Zone Zero
Zenless Zone Zero is an urban fantasy action RPG developed by HoYoverse. Players explore a futuristic city, battling otherworldly beings known as Hollows. The game features a dynamic combat system, allowing players to switch between characters and unleash powerful combos. It also includes a gacha system for acquiring characters and weapons.
Genshin Impact
Genshin Impact is an open-world action RPG set in the fantasy world of Teyvat. Players embark on a journey to find their lost sibling, exploring diverse regions, battling enemies, and solving puzzles. The game features a gacha system for obtaining characters and weapons, each with unique elemental abilities. The stunning visuals, engaging storyline, and vast open world make Genshin Impact a popular choice for many players.
Blue Archive
Blue Archive is a tactical RPG set in the academic city of Kivotos. As a teacher, you lead a team of students to solve various incidents and mysteries. The game features turn-based combat, character customization, and a gacha system to recruit new students. The game's unique blend of heartwarming stories, strategic gameplay, and stunning anime-style visuals has made it a popular choice for many players.

About me playing Gacha Games

I love playing gacha games and enjoy the excitement of summoning new characters or items. Games like Genshin Impact, Honkai: Star Rail, Wuthering Waves, and Punishing: Gray Raven are some of my favorites. I like saving resources for special pulls, building strong teams, and exploring the amazing stories and worlds these games offer. Whether I get lucky or not, every pull is an adventure, and I enjoy sharing those moments with others.
